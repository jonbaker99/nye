# New Year's Eve 2025 Cocktail Menu Project

## Overview
Created an elegant cocktail menu for a NYE 2025 house party with limited home bar ingredients.

## Available Ingredients
**Spirits & Fortified Wines:**
- Gin, Vodka, Aperol, Dry vermouth, Sweet (red) vermouth

**Mixers & Bubbly:**
- Champagne, Tonic water, Coke

**Juices & Citrus:**
- Orange juice, Apple juice, Lemons, Limes

## Final House Specials (4 champagne-focused cocktails)
1. **New Year's Spritz** *(Aperol Spritz)* - Available without champagne
2. **Appley New Year!** *(Apple 75)* - Gin, lemon, apple juice, champagne
3. **New-Year-Groni Sbagliato*** *(Negroni Sbagliato)* - Can be made as regular Negroni
4. **Sparkling 2026 Sunrise*** *(Sparkling Greyhound)* - Can be made as regular Greyhound

*Asterisk indicates available without champagne on request*

## Key Design Decisions
- Champagne-focused specials for celebration vibe
- Balanced variety: 2 Aperol-based, 1 gin, 1 vodka
- Simple, punny New Year's naming
- All "prosecco" references changed to "champagne" for elegance
- Ingredient list separated from description text for clarity

## Deliverable
Single-page HTML menu with elegant typography (Cormorant Garamond + Montserrat), featuring house specials at top and full categorized cocktail list below.

## Design Aesthetic
- Understated, classy fonts
- Minimal decoration (gold accent line)
- Clean spacing and hierarchy
- Print and web optimized
